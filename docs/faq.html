Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer private tutoring or live instruction?

No. Private instruction is not available for this course. The time commitment required to deliver personalized tutoring falls below a viable rate for the instructor. However, the entire course is freely available as self-study material, and video versions of all lessons will be published on YouTube with the same structured curriculum.

Can I use your course materials for my own projects?

Yes. This course is released under the MIT License. You are free to use, copy, modify, and distribute the materials for any purpose, including commercial use, provided that appropriate credit is given to the original author. A link back to this course or a mention of the source is appreciated.

I have questions about the course content. How can I get help?

For questions, discussion, and deeper engagement with the material, join the community on Patreon:

https://www.patreon.com/c/hevangel

Patreon supporters get access to Q&A threads, additional commentary, and the opportunity to suggest topics for future lessons and side content.

Why did you create this course?

This course originated from the author's personal study notes accumulated over years of self-directed investment education, including CFA curriculum materials. When organizing these notes with AI assistance, the result was comprehensive enough to share as a structured course. The research was already done, the tokens were already spent -- it made more sense to publish it than to keep it private. If these materials help others avoid common mistakes and build better portfolios, that is a worthwhile outcome.
