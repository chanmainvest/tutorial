Level 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-12)

Part 1: Reading Section

Level Overview

Level 1 is your entry point into the world of investing. Over twelve weeks, you will build a solid foundation in the principles that every successful investor understands. No prior knowledge of finance or investing is required.

What You Will Learn

This level covers the essential building blocks:

Weeks 1-4: Core Concepts

  • Why investing is necessary and how compound interest works
  • Index funds and ETFs as your simplest, most effective starting tools
  • The fundamental relationship between risk and return
  • The classic 60/40 portfolio and why it has been the default for decades

Weeks 5-8: Asset Classes and Analysis

  • How bonds work and why they stabilize your portfolio
  • Gold and commodities as diversifiers
  • The discipline of portfolio rebalancing
  • Reading financial statements -- the three essential reports

Weeks 9-12: Market Context and Your First Portfolio

  • How market indexes are built and what they measure
  • Economic cycles and how they affect different asset classes
  • Behavioral biases -- the psychological traps that hurt investors
  • Putting it all together to build your first real portfolio

What You Will Be Able to Do After Level 1

By the end of these twelve weeks, you will be able to:

  • Explain the time value of money and compound interest to anyone
  • Open a brokerage account and buy your first index fund or ETF
  • Build a simple but effective portfolio with proper asset allocation
  • Read the three core financial statements at a basic level
  • Recognize and resist common behavioral biases in your investment decisions
  • Understand where you are in the economic cycle and what that means for your portfolio

Who This Level Is For

Level 1 is designed for complete beginners. If you have never invested before, or if you have been investing without fully understanding why, this level gives you the foundation to make informed decisions. Even experienced investors benefit from revisiting these fundamentals -- the basics are where the biggest mistakes happen.

第5至8週：資產類別與分析

  • 債券的運作原理，以及它如何穩定你的投資組合
  • 黃金與商品作為分散投資的工具
  • 投資組合再平衡的紀律

  • 融會貫通，建立你的第一個真正的投資組合

完成第一級後你將能夠做到的事

  • 向任何人清楚解釋貨幣時間價值與複利的概念

  • 以妥善的資產配置建立一個簡單而有效的投資組合
  • 以基礎水平閱讀三份核心財務報表
  • 識別並抵抗投資決策中常見的行為偏差
  • 了解自身在經濟週期中所處的位置，以及這對你的投資組合意味著什麼

本級別的適合對象

第一級專為完全初學者而設。如果你從未投資過，或者你一直在投資但未能完全理解背後的原因，本級別將為你提供作出明智決策所需的基礎。即使是經驗豐富的投資者，重溫這些基礎知識同樣受益匪淺——最大的錯誤往往就出在基本功上。
