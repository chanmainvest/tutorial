Four US balance-sheet series in trillions of USD on the same axis. Toggle each on or off and switch between linear and log scale. Hover for a year-by-year crosshair showing every visible series at that year.

Note on M1. The Fed redefined M1 in May 2020 to include savings deposits, which produces the visible step-up in the M1 line that year. The jump is a definition change, not a real-world money-creation event of that size. Note on private debt. "US private debt" here is household debt + non-financial business credit-market debt from the Fed's Z.1 release. Financial-sector debt is excluded.
