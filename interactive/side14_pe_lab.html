Stylised PE fund cash flow. Capital calls front-load through years 1-5 (fixed pacing 20/25/22/18/15%); distributions back-load through years 4-10 scaled to hit your gross IRR target. Management fees accrue at the slider rate on commitments years 1-5 and on declining NAV thereafter; carried interest is 20% of profits above an 8% hurdle (with catch-up). Truncating the horizon below 10 years cuts off later distributions, magnifying the J-curve and reducing net IRR. Negative net IRRs are clipped on display.
