Level 5: Expert (Weeks 47-52)

Part 1: Reading Section

Level Overview

Level 5 is the capstone. Over six weeks, you will master the most advanced portfolio strategies and bring everything together into a complete, professional-grade investment framework. These are the techniques used by endowment funds, family offices, and top-tier portfolio managers.

What You Will Learn

Weeks 47-49: Advanced Strategies

  • Tail risk hedging -- protecting against black swan events
  • Structured products and defined-outcome investments
  • Volatility arbitrage and exploiting the variance risk premium

Weeks 50-52: Portfolio Integration

  • Factor tilts and alternative risk premia -- harvesting multiple systematic return sources
  • Managed futures and trend following -- crisis alpha that profits when everything else crashes
  • Complete portfolio integration -- assembling an all-weather portfolio

What You Will Be Able to Do After Level 5

  • Hedge your portfolio against extreme market events without excessive cost
  • Evaluate structured products and understand their embedded options
  • Exploit volatility term structure for additional returns
  • Construct a factor-tilted portfolio that targets specific risk premia
  • Understand trend-following strategies and their role as crisis alpha
  • Build a complete all-weather portfolio that performs across different market regimes

第5級：專家（第47-52週）

第一部分：閱讀章節

級別概覽

第5級是整個課程的壓軸。在六週內，你將掌握最進階的投資組合策略，並將所有知識融會貫通，建立一套完整的專業級投資框架。這些正是捐贈基金、家族辦公室及頂尖投資組合經理所採用的技術。

學習內容

第47-49週：進階策略

  • 尾部風險對沖——防範黑天鵝事件
  • 結構性產品與定義回報投資
  • 波動率套利及利用方差風險溢價

第50-52週：投資組合整合

  • 因子傾斜與另類風險溢價——收割多種系統性回報來源
  • 管理期貨與趨勢跟蹤——在市場全線崩潰時仍能獲利的危機阿爾法
  • 完整投資組合整合——構建全天候投資組合

完成第5級後，你將能夠

  • 以合理成本對沖投資組合，抵禦極端市場事件
  • 評估結構性產品，並理解其內嵌期權
  • 利用波動率期限結構獲取額外回報
  • 構建具備因子傾斜的投資組合，針對特定風險溢價
  • 理解趨勢跟蹤策略及其作為危機阿爾法的角色
  • 建立一個能夠在不同市場週期下穩健運作的全天候投資組合
