Yield-on-cost = starting yield × (1 + growth)^N. Cumulative dividends sum the year-by-year cash payment from year 1 to year N (no reinvestment); annual cash = initial × y0 × (1+g)^(t-1). Tax: qualified dividends use the LTCG schedule (12% bracket = 0%, 22-32% = 15%, 37% = 20%) plus state rate; ordinary dividends use the full bracket plus state. Share-price growth is held flat for clarity — the point is to isolate the income compounding, not the total return.
