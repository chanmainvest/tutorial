Approximate annual aggregates of the Kenneth French five-factor + momentum series, 1963-2024. Weights sum to 100%; the blend backtest takes a weighted sum of the five academic long-short factor returns each year, compounds it, and reports geometric return, volatility, Sharpe (mean/std), and max drawdown. Pure Value flatlines after 2007. Pure Momentum has the 2009 cliff. The diversified blends ride above any single factor's drawdown.
